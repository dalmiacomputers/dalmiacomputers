Heritage & Culture

Explore natural landscapes, hills and reservoirs, folk performances, terracotta traditions and historic estates that shaped the region’s identity. Travel responsibly, support local artisans and plan trips during pleasant seasons.

  • Main attractions: hill viewpoints, waterfalls, lakes, eco‑parks and heritage temples.
  • Culture: folk dance traditions, instrumental rhythms, craftwork and seasonal fairs.
  • Travel tips: book trains early; carry ID, cashless payments; respect local customs.
